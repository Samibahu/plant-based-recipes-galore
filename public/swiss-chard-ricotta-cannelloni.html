Swiss Chard and Ricotta Cannelloni

Ingredients

  • 12 cannelloni tubes
  • 2 cups Swiss chard, chopped
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup marinara sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

Cooking Process

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the cannelloni according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat. Add Swiss chard and cook until wilted. Let cool.
  4. In a bowl, combine the cooked chard with ricotta cheese, Parmesan cheese, oregano, and basil. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Stuff the cannelloni tubes with the chard and ricotta mixture.
  6. Spread a thin layer of marinara sauce in a baking dish. Arrange the stuffed cannelloni on top.
  7. Cover with the remaining marinara sauce and sprinkle with additional Parmesan cheese if desired.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until the cannelloni are cooked through and the sauce is bubbling.
  9. Let cool slightly before serving.

Nutritional Information

Serving Size: 1 serving (2 cannelloni tubes)

Calories: 350 kcal

Protein: 15 g

Carbohydrates: 40 g

Fat: 15 g

Sodium: 600 mg

Cooking Tips and Tricks

1. For a richer flavor, consider adding a pinch of nutmeg to the ricotta mixture.

2. If the cannelloni are too difficult to stuff, you can pre-cook them for a few minutes until slightly softened.

3. Make sure the marinara sauce covers the cannelloni completely to prevent them from drying out during baking.

4. For added texture and flavor, top with a mix of breadcrumbs and Parmesan cheese before baking.

5. Let the cannelloni rest for a few minutes before serving to allow the filling to set.
