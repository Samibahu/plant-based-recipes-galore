Kale Pesto Pasta

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces pasta (such as penne or fusilli)
  • 2 cups kale, stems removed and leaves chopped
  • 1/2 cup pine nuts
  • 1/4 cup nutritional yeast
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Cooking Process

  1. Cook the pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a food processor, combine kale, pine nuts, nutritional yeast, and garlic. Pulse until finely chopped.
  3. With the processor running, gradually add olive oil and lemon juice until the mixture forms a smooth paste. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Toss the cooked pasta with the kale pesto until well coated.
  5. Serve with grated Parmesan cheese if desired.

Nutritional Information

Serving Size: 1 serving (about 1 cup)

Calories: 350 kcal

Protein: 8 g

Carbohydrates: 40 g

Fat: 18 g

Sodium: 250 mg

Cooking Tips and Tricks

1. For a richer flavor, lightly toast the pine nuts before adding them to the pesto.

2. Use a high-quality olive oil for the best taste.

3. If the pesto is too thick, add a bit of pasta cooking water to reach the desired consistency.

4. Make a double batch of pesto and freeze it for future use. It keeps well in the freezer for up to 3 months.
