Lavender Shortbread Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons dried lavender flowers
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 325°F (163°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a bowl, cream together the butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  3. Mix in the flour and salt until just combined. Fold in the lavender flowers.
  4. Roll the dough into a log and chill for 30 minutes.
  5. Slice the dough into 1/4-inch thick rounds and place them on the prepared baking sheet.
  6. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the edges are lightly golden.
  7. Let the cookies cool on a wire rack before serving.

Nutritional Information

Serving Size: 1 cookie (1/24 of batch)

Calories: 140 kcal

Protein: 1 g

Carbohydrates: 18 g

Fat: 7 g

Sodium: 50 mg

Cooking Tips and Tricks

1. Make sure the butter is fully softened to ensure a smooth dough. If it's too cold, the cookies may be tough.

2. For uniform cookies, use a cookie cutter or slice the dough with a sharp knife.

3. If you want to add extra flavor, consider mixing in a teaspoon of vanilla extract.

4. Chill the dough thoroughly before baking to help the cookies retain their shape and avoid spreading too much.

5. Store the cookies in an airtight container to keep them fresh for up to a week. You can also freeze them for up to 3 months.
