Mint Chocolate Mousse

Ingredients

  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon peppermint extract
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. In a small saucepan, heat the cream over medium heat until it begins to simmer.
  2. Remove from heat and add the chocolate chips, stirring until smooth and melted.
  3. Stir in the peppermint extract and a pinch of salt.
  4. Let the mixture cool to room temperature, then refrigerate until chilled.
  5. Once chilled, whip the mixture with an electric mixer until light and fluffy.
  6. Transfer to serving dishes and refrigerate for at least 1 hour before serving.

Nutritional Information

Serving Size: 1/2 cup

Calories: 200 kcal

Protein: 2 g

Carbohydrates: 20 g

Fat: 14 g

Sodium: 40 mg

Cooking Tips and Tricks

1. For a richer chocolate flavor, use high-quality semi-sweet chocolate chips or chopped chocolate.

2. Make sure the cream is fully chilled before whipping for the best texture.

3. If you prefer a stronger mint flavor, increase the peppermint extract slightly.

4. To avoid over-whipping the cream, watch closely as it begins to thicken and stop as soon as soft peaks form.

5. Garnish with shaved chocolate or fresh mint leaves for a decorative touch before serving.
