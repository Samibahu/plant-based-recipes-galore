Curry Leaves Upma

Ingredients

  • 1 cup semolina (rava)
  • 1/2 cup curry leaves, finely chopped
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 green chilies, slit
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 tablespoon urad dal
  • 1 tablespoon chana dal
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 2 cups water

Cooking Process

  1. Dry roast the semolina in a pan until it turns light golden. Set aside.
  2. In the same pan, heat oil and add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, urad dal, and chana dal. Sauté until the dals turn golden brown.
  3. Add the chopped onion, green chilies, and curry leaves. Sauté until the onions turn translucent.
  4. Pour in the water and add salt. Bring to a boil.
  5. Gradually add the roasted semolina while stirring continuously to avoid lumps.
  6. Cook on low heat until the upma thickens and the semolina is cooked through. Serve hot with coconut chutney or pickle.
