Dhaniya Chutney

Ingredients

  • 1 cup fresh coriander leaves
  • 2 green chilies
  • 1-inch piece of ginger
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed

Instructions

  1. In a blender, combine coriander leaves, green chilies, ginger, lemon juice, and salt.
  2. Add a small amount of water and blend until smooth. Adjust the consistency by adding more water if needed.
  3. Taste and adjust seasoning if necessary.
  4. Transfer the chutney to a serving bowl. Serve with snacks like fritters or parathas.

Nutritional Information

Serving Size: 2 tablespoons

Calories: 30 kcal

Protein: 1 g

Carbohydrates: 6 g

Fat: 0.5 g

Sodium: 150 mg

Cooking Tips and Tricks

1. For a tangier flavor, you can add an extra tablespoon of lemon juice.

2. Adjust the spiciness by varying the number of green chilies used.

3. If you prefer a smoother chutney, blend the ingredients for a longer time or use a food processor.

4. Store the chutney in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to a week.

5. For a variation, add a small handful of mint leaves for a refreshing twist.
