Sautéed Swiss Chard

Ingredients

  • 1 bunch Swiss chard, stems removed and leaves chopped
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice

Cooking Process

  1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add minced garlic and red pepper flakes, and cook until fragrant.
  2. Add the Swiss chard and cook, stirring occasionally, until wilted and tender, about 5 minutes.
  3. Season with salt, pepper, and lemon juice. Serve warm.

Nutritional Information

Serving Size: 1 serving (about 1 cup)

Calories: 120 kcal

Protein: 3 g

Carbohydrates: 10 g

Fat: 9 g

Sodium: 200 mg

Cooking Tips and Tricks

1. For a deeper flavor, try adding a splash of balsamic vinegar along with the lemon juice.

2. If you prefer a milder garlic flavor, sauté the garlic for a shorter time or use roasted garlic instead.

3. To enhance the texture, consider adding toasted pine nuts or slivered almonds for a bit of crunch.

4. Make sure not to overcook the Swiss chard to retain its vibrant color and nutrients.
