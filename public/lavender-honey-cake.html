Lavender Honey Cake

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup honey
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 tablespoon dried lavender flowers
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour an 8-inch round cake pan.
  2. In a bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In another bowl, cream together the butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  4. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then mix in the honey.
  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, alternating with the milk, beginning and ending with the dry ingredients.
  6. Fold in the lavender flowers.
  7. Pour the batter into the prepared cake pan and smooth the top.
  8. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  9. Let the cake cool in the pan for 10 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.

Nutritional Information

Serving Size: 1 slice (1/12 of cake)

Calories: 320 kcal

Protein: 4 g

Carbohydrates: 45 g

Fat: 15 g

Sodium: 180 mg

Cooking Tips and Tricks

1. Ensure the butter is softened to room temperature for easier creaming with the sugar.

2. For a more intense lavender flavor, steep the lavender flowers in the milk for 10 minutes before using.

3. If the cake domes while baking, use a serrated knife to level the top before frosting or serving.

4. You can add a simple glaze made with powdered sugar and lemon juice for extra sweetness and a shiny finish.

5. Store the cake in an airtight container to keep it fresh for up to 5 days.
