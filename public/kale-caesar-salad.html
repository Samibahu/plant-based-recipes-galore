Kale Caesar Salad

Ingredients

  • 1 bunch kale, stems removed and leaves chopped
  • 1 cup croutons
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese (optional)
  • 1/2 cup Caesar dressing (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Cooking Process

  1. In a large bowl, massage the kale with olive oil and a pinch of salt until the leaves are tender.
  2. Add minced garlic and toss to combine.
  3. Pour Caesar dressing over the kale and toss well to coat the leaves evenly.
  4. Top with croutons and grated Parmesan cheese, if using.
  5. Serve immediately or chill in the refrigerator for 15 minutes before serving.

Nutritional Information

Serving Size: 1 serving (about 1 cup)

Calories: 200 kcal

Protein: 5 g

Carbohydrates: 20 g

Fat: 12 g

Sodium: 350 mg

Cooking Tips and Tricks

1. Massage the kale well with olive oil to make it tender and reduce bitterness.

2. Use stale bread for croutons for better texture, or make your own by baking cubes of bread with olive oil and seasoning.

3. For a creamier dressing, you can blend homemade Caesar dressing ingredients in a food processor.

4. If making ahead, keep the dressing and croutons separate until ready to serve to prevent sogginess.
