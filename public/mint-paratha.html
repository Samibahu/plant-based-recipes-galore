Mint Paratha

Ingredients

  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 1/2 cup fresh mint leaves, finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • Water as needed
  • Ghee or oil for cooking

Instructions

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the wheat flour, chopped mint leaves, cumin seeds, salt, and turmeric powder.
  2. Add water gradually and knead into a soft dough.
  3. Divide the dough into equal portions and roll each into a ball.
  4. Roll each ball into a flat circle, dusting with flour as needed.
  5. Heat a griddle or tawa over medium heat. Cook each paratha, applying ghee or oil on both sides until golden brown.
  6. Serve hot with yogurt or a pickle.

Nutritional Information

Serving Size: 1 paratha

Calories: 150 kcal

Protein: 4 g

Carbohydrates: 30 g

Fat: 2 g

Sodium: 200 mg

Cooking Tips and Tricks

1. For extra flavor, you can add a pinch of garam masala or chili powder to the dough.

2. Ensure the dough is soft but not sticky; adding water gradually helps achieve the right consistency.

3. Use a rolling pin to evenly roll out the parathas to ensure even cooking.

4. Cook the parathas on a hot griddle to get a crispy texture. If using ghee, it adds a richer flavor.

5. You can also stuff the parathas with additional ingredients like paneer or potatoes for a variation.
