Mint Chocolate Chip Ice Cream

Ingredients

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup fresh mint leaves
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. In a saucepan, heat the milk, sugar, and salt over medium heat, stirring until the sugar is dissolved.
  2. Add the mint leaves and bring to a simmer. Remove from heat and let steep for 30 minutes.
  3. Strain out the mint leaves and stir in the cream and vanilla extract.
  4. Chill the mixture in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours.
  5. Churn the mixture in an ice cream maker according to the manufacturerâ€™s instructions.
  6. In the last few minutes of churning, add the chocolate chips.
  7. Transfer to a lidded container and freeze until firm.

Nutritional Information

Serving Size: 1/2 cup

Calories: 250 kcal

Protein: 3 g

Carbohydrates: 24 g

Fat: 17 g

Sodium: 70 mg

Cooking Tips and Tricks

1. Use fresh mint leaves for the best flavor. If using dried mint, adjust the quantity accordingly.

2. To enhance the mint flavor, you can infuse the mint leaves in the cream mixture for longer.

3. Ensure that the ice cream mixture is fully chilled before churning to achieve a smoother texture.

4. If you don't have an ice cream maker, you can place the mixture in a lidded container and stir it every 30 minutes as it freezes to break up ice crystals.

5. For a creamier texture, consider adding a small amount of corn syrup or glycerin to the mixture.
