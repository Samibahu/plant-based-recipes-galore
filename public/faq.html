Frequently Asked Questions

What are the benefits of adding fenugreek to plant-based recipes?
▼

Fenugreek is known for its health benefits including improving digestion, lowering blood sugar levels, and reducing inflammation. It adds a unique, slightly bitter flavor to dishes.

How can I use coriander in plant-based snacks?
▼

Coriander is commonly used in snacks like coriander chutney and coriander paratha. It adds a fresh, zesty flavor to various dishes and pairs well with other spices.

What are some ways to use kale in plant-based dishes?
▼

Kale can be used in salads, smoothies, or cooked dishes like kale pesto pasta. It’s packed with nutrients, including fiber and vitamins A and C, making it a great addition to plant-based meals.

How can mint be used in savory and sweet plant-based dishes?
▼

Mint is versatile and can be used in savory dishes like mint paratha and chutney, or sweet desserts like mint chocolate mousse and ice cream. Its refreshing flavor makes it ideal for a variety of dishes.

What are the nutritional benefits of spinach in plant-based recipes?
▼

Spinach is rich in iron, calcium, and vitamins A and C. It’s an excellent ingredient for plant-based meals and can be used in a variety of dishes like curries, salads, and smoothies.

How can I incorporate lavender in plant-based desserts?
▼

Lavender adds a floral note to desserts and is best used sparingly. Try it in recipes like lavender shortbread cookies, lavender ice cream, or honey lavender cake for a unique flavor profile.

Can mint be used for more than just garnishing dishes?
▼

Absolutely! Mint is a key ingredient in many dishes beyond just garnish. It is used in chutneys, drinks, parathas, and even in desserts like mint chocolate chip ice cream.

How do I prepare Swiss chard for plant-based dishes?
▼

Swiss chard can be sautéed with garlic and olive oil, or used in casseroles like Swiss chard and ricotta cannelloni. It has a slightly bitter flavor and is rich in vitamins and minerals.

What are some plant-based alternatives to paneer in palak paneer?
▼

For a plant-based version of palak paneer, you can substitute paneer with tofu or cashew-based paneer. These alternatives work well with the creamy spinach sauce, maintaining the dish's traditional flavors.

How can I grow fresh herbs like mint and coriander at home?
▼

Mint and coriander are easy to grow at home. Mint grows best in pots to prevent spreading, while coriander needs well-drained soil and plenty of sunlight. Regular watering and pruning will help them thrive.
