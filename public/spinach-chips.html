Spinach Chips

Ingredients

  • 1 bunch spinach leaves, washed and dried
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Optional: Garlic powder, onion powder, or other seasonings

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a large bowl, toss the spinach leaves with olive oil, ensuring they are evenly coated.
  3. Arrange the leaves in a single layer on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  4. Sprinkle with salt and any additional seasonings if desired.
  5. Bake for 10-15 minutes, or until the leaves are crispy.
  6. Remove from the oven and let cool. Serve as a crunchy snack.

Nutritional Information

Serving Size: 1 serving (about 1 cup)

Calories: 50 kcal

Protein: 1 g

Carbohydrates: 3 g

Fat: 4 g

Sodium: 100 mg

Cooking Tips and Tricks

1. Make sure the spinach leaves are completely dry before tossing with oil. Any moisture can cause them to become soggy instead of crispy.

2. For even crispiness, avoid overlapping the leaves on the baking sheet. If necessary, use multiple sheets or bake in batches.

3. Adjust the seasoning according to your taste. Experiment with different spices like paprika, cumin, or chili powder for a flavor twist.

4. Keep an eye on the spinach chips while baking, as they can quickly go from crispy to burnt.

5. Store any leftover chips in an airtight container at room temperature to keep them crisp.
