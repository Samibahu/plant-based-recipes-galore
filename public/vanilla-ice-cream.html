Vanilla Ice Cream

Ingredients

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Cooking Process

  1. In a mixing bowl, whisk together the heavy cream, milk, sugar, vanilla extract, and salt until the sugar is dissolved.
  2. Pour the mixture into an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer's instructions.
  3. Transfer the churned ice cream to a container and freeze for at least 2 hours before serving.

Nutritional Information

Serving Size: 1/2 cup

Calories: 250 kcal

Protein: 3 g

Carbohydrates: 23 g

Fat: 17 g

Sodium: 60 mg

Cooking Tips and Tricks

1. For the creamiest texture, make sure your ice cream maker is well chilled before use.

2. If you don't have an ice cream maker, you can freeze the mixture in a shallow dish and stir every 30 minutes until frozen.

3. To add mix-ins like chocolate chips or fruit, fold them in during the last 5 minutes of churning.

4. Let the ice cream sit at room temperature for a few minutes before scooping for easier serving.

5. Store ice cream in an airtight container to prevent ice crystals from forming.
